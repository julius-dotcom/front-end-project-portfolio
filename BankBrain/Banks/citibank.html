Citibank Codes

All essential USSD codes and instructions for Citibank.

Account Management

  • Check Balance: *222#
  • Account Statement: *222*0#
  • Block Card: Call 0700-222-000
  • Open New Account: Visit branch or app

Transfers & Payments

  • Transfer to Own Account: *222*Recipient*Amount#
  • Transfer to Other Banks: *222*BankCode*Recipient*Amount#
  • Pay Bills: *222*BillerCode*Amount#
  • Buy Airtime/Data: *222*Amount#

Cards & Loans

  • Activate Card: Call customer care
  • Request Loan Info: Visit branch or app

Bank Services

  • Customer Care: 0700-222-000
  • Locate ATM/Branch: Use app or website
  • Request Cheque Book: Call or app

Tips & Security

  • Keep your PIN confidential
  • Avoid public Wi-Fi for banking
  • Verify recipient before transfers
