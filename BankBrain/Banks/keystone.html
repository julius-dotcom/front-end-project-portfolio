Keystone Bank Codes

All essential USSD codes and instructions for Keystone Bank.

How to Use USSD Banking

  • Dial: *7111# from your bank-registered phone number.
  • Enter: your NUBAN account number.
  • Create: a PIN if prompted.
  • Use: the PIN to authenticate any financial transaction.

Common USSD Codes

  • Balance Enquiry: *7111*00#
  • Airtime Self-Recharge: *7111*amount#
  • Airtime for Others: *7111*amount*PhoneNumber#
  • Data Top-Up: *7111*5#
  • Panic Code (Freeze Account): *7111*911#

Tips & Security

  • Keep your PIN confidential and never share it.
  • Always verify recipient account before transfers.
  • Avoid using public Wi-Fi for USSD banking.
  • Contact Keystone Bank customer care if suspicious activity occurs.
